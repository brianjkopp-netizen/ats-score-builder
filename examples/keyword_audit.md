`before_resume.md`. It is the bridge that explains every change in `after_resume.md`.

## Step 3 — Acronyms: both forms required

Each must appear spelled out AND abbreviated at least once:

- Return on Investment (ROI)
- Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Artificial Intelligence (AI)
- Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC)

## Soft skills to weave into the summary (Step 4)

cross-functional collaboration, stakeholder management, communication, continuous improvement,
customer focus.

## Formatting fixes flagged (Steps 7–11)

- Em dash in the summary → replace with a comma or period.
- Long-form dates ("January 2021 to Present") → MM/YYYY ("01/2021 – Present").
- Pipe-separated skills are fine as commas; keep pipes only in the header line.
- Bold is currently on company names only, which is correct — keep it that way, add nothing.

## Non-addressable gaps (do NOT fabricate)

None in this example — Jordan genuinely has agile, roadmap, ML/AI, and cross-functional experience;
the issue is purely wording and formatting. If the JD had required, say, FedRAMP authorization
experience Jordan lacked, that would be flagged here as a non-addressable gap and left out, not
invented.
