# Sample Job Description — Senior Product Manager, Digital Platforms

**Company:** Northwind Health (fictional, for illustration)
**Location:** Minneapolis, MN
**Title:** Senior Product Manager, Digital Platforms

## About the role

Northwind Health is hiring a Senior Product Manager, Digital Platforms to own the product
strategy and roadmap for our member-facing digital platform. You will lead cross-functional
collaboration across engineering, design, and marketing to deliver measurable business outcomes.
This role requires strong stakeholder management, a bias toward continuous improvement, and the
ability to translate Artificial Intelligence (AI) capabilities into customer value.

## Responsibilities

- Own the product roadmap for our digital platform and drive measurable Return on Investment (ROI)
- Lead cross-functional collaboration across engineering, design, marketing, and clinical operations
- Define and track Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) for product success
- Champion Agile software development practices and continuous improvement across product teams
- Partner with data teams to embed Artificial Intelligence (AI) into the member experience
- Manage stakeholder relationships and communicate roadmap decisions with clarity
- Drive customer focus through user research, wireframes, and rapid prototyping
- Own the end-to-end Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC) for platform features

## Qualifications

- 7+ years of product management experience, ideally in healthcare or a regulated industry
- Demonstrated experience driving Return on Investment (ROI) through digital products
- Strong track record of cross-functional collaboration and stakeholder management
- Hands-on experience with Agile software development and the Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC)
- Experience defining Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and using data to drive decisions
- Familiarity with Artificial Intelligence (AI) and machine learning product applications
- Excellent communication skills and a bias toward continuous improvement
- Bachelor's degree required; MBA preferred

## What success looks like

In your first year, you will own the platform roadmap, establish KPIs, deliver ROI through
shipped features, and build a culture of continuous improvement and cross-functional collaboration.
